What is a P/E ratio and why does it matter?
Understanding stock valuation fundamentals
BeginnerStocksNGX
⏱ 5 min read+100 pts on completion
📖 Quick lesson
The Price-to-Earnings (P/E) ratio tells you how much investors are paying for each naira of a company's earnings. A P/E of 10x means investors pay ₦10 for every ₦1 the company earns. Higher P/E = higher growth expectations. Nigerian banks like GTCO typically trade at 3–6x P/E, while growth stocks globally trade at 20–40x.
